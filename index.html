NGS Benchmark Data Explorer

This interactive map displays National Geodetic Survey (NGS) benchmark data across the United States. Each marker represents a geodetic control point with precise coordinates used for surveying and mapping applications.

Features:

  • Search: Find the nearest benchmark to any address
  • Filter: Display specific types of geodetic control points
  • Explore: Click markers to view detailed datasheet information
  • Navigate: Pan and zoom to explore different regions

Data Types: Horizontal control points provide latitude/longitude coordinates, vertical control points provide elevation data, CORS stations offer real-time GPS corrections, and gravity stations support geophysical studies.

Data Source: National Geodetic Survey (NGS) - NOAA's National Ocean Service
